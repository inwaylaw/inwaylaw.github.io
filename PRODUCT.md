# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Investors, collaborators, early customers, partners, and peers who want to quickly understand the founder's AI product direction, judgment, and taste. They are likely scanning on desktop before a meeting or on mobile after receiving a link.

## Product Purpose

Present an AI startup founder through the theme AI for Love: AI systems that earn trust in real human contexts. Success means the page feels warm, credible, precise, and memorable while still making the founder's product thesis and representative work easy to grasp.

## Brand Personality

Warm, restrained, trustworthy. The voice should feel human and thoughtful, with a founder's clarity rather than a template site's performance.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS gradients, cold cyberpunk AI visuals, overused glassmorphism, crowded card grids, loud decorative motion, and portfolio templates that feel interchangeable. Avoid turning Van Gogh references into literal painting cosplay; keep the influence in color, rhythm, and atmosphere.

## Design Principles

- Love is operational: show care through trust, confirmation, evidence, and human judgment.
- Warmth without softness: keep the page inviting while preserving founder-level decisiveness.
- Art informs the system: borrow Van Gogh's color relationships and brush movement, not literal pastiche.
- Motion should breathe: dynamic effects must feel alive but quiet enough for reading.
- Proof over spectacle: every visual flourish should support credibility and product clarity.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Aim for readable contrast and responsive behavior across desktop and mobile. Motion must respect reduced-motion preferences. Decorative canvas and texture layers should remain non-essential and hidden from assistive technology.
